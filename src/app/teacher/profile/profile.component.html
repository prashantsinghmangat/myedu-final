About Me

Hello, I'm Das Gunjan, and I am deeply passionate about music. As the proud founder of my very own Institute of Music located in Delhi, I have had the privilege of immersing myself in the world of melodies and rhythms. My journey in the world of music began at the renowned Delhi School of Music, where I honed my skills as a guitarist and developed a profound appreciation for the art form.
