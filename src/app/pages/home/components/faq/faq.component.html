Frequently asked questions

Why should I use TIL?

"When it comes to learning, the benefits of regular learning cannot be overstated. By committing to just a small amount of learning each day, we can see incredible progress over time."

Today I Learned- is a great way to keep track of what you learn. It's also a great way to share your knowledge with others. You can use it as a personal journal or as a blog.

Compared to other blogging platforms, TIL is very simple and lightweight. It's also free and open source. You can start writing immediately without having to worry about setting up a blog. Just sign up and start writing!

How do I get started?

Just sign up and start writing! You can sign up using LinkedIn, Google, or GitHub. Immediately after signing up, you can write a post and start your new daily learning habit. TIL is designed social media in mind, so you can share your posts with others and get feedback from them.

Is my data safe with TIL?

Yes, your data is safe with TIL. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We also have a strict privacy policy that we follow. You can read more about it here: Privacy Policy

I found a bug, how do I report it?

If you find a bug, please report it here: GitHub Issues. We will fix it as soon as possible. If you have any other questions or concerns, please contact us at: contact@learnedtoday.app
